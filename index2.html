❗ Quiz Me! - Lead Developer

Quiz Me! is a 1st party voice-forward trivia skill powered by Alexa's own Knowledge Graph. Players can test their knowledge across thousands of dynamically generated and engaging categories. Featured at the 2019 Game Developers Conference. Don't just take my word for it. Here's what one 😄 customer had to say:



One of the best Alexa skill games so far

I really like this game. We plan it on long drives-- so traffic doesn't bother us as much. I love movie trivia and there are lots of variations in this game-- as well as a lot of other types of categories. I only wish there was a list of the available categories somewhere. I figured out that I can say: "Quiz me on movies from the 1930's" (or 40's, 50's, etc. or you can ask to be quizzed on movies from specific directors or specific actors. In a couple of instances the skill had some issues: a few times it read the questions in Spanish instead of english and sometimes it says it does not have a category that I know it has because I've played it already. But the problems are not often.

Cheers, Amy

📙 The Dictionary Game - Creator

The Dictionary Game, the popular parlor game, can now be played online with friends! Trick your friends into guessing for your made-up definition to an obscure word and see if you can spot the correct definition among the crowd. Players can use their phones, tablets or laptops to play together in real-time. At least 2 players are required to play, but the more players join the harder and crazier it becomes to get your friends to vote for your definition. Entrant in the 2018 International Game Festival.
