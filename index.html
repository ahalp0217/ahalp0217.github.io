The Dictionary Game, the popular parlor game, can now be played online with friends! Trick your friends into guessing for your made-up definition to an obscure word and see if you can spot the correct definition among the crowd. Players can use their phones, tablets or laptops to play together in real-time. At least 2 players are required to play, but the more players join the harder and crazier it becomes to get your friends to vote for your definition.
