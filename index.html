Creator

The Dictionary Game, the popular parlor game, can now be played online with friends! Trick your friends into guessing for your made-up definition to an obscure word and see if you can spot the correct definition among the crowd. Players can use their phones, tablets or laptops to play together in real-time. At least 2 players are required to play, but the more players join the harder and crazier it becomes to get your friends to vote for your definition. Unbalanced
Lead Designer

Unbalanced is a side-view action puzzle game where players must strategically move around an ever-tilting world in order to restore balance. The catch: everything in the game has weight. Players must figure out how to balance a board with moving enemies, objects, and even their own weight as they traverse the levels. Players will have visual feedback with the physical tilt of the board and meter on the X axis that indicate if the board is successfully balanced.
