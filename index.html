DictionaryGame.io
Creator

The Dictionary Game, a popular parlor game, can now be played online with friends! Players can use their phones, tablets or laptops to play together in real-time. At least 2 players are required to play, but the more players join the harder and crazier it becomes to get your friends to vote for your definition. Entrant in the 2018 International Game Festival.

Unbalanced
Lead Designer

Unbalanced is a side-view action puzzle game where players must strategically move around an ever-tilting world in order to restore balance. The catch: everything in the game has weight. Players must figure out how to balance a board with moving enemies, objects, and even their own weight as they traverse the levels. Players will have visual feedback with the physical tilt of the board and meter on the X axis that indicate if the board is successfully balanced. Controlling a handy robot, BEE-3, players can shock enemies and move weighted objects to change the board’s tilt to balance the level.

Rhythm Knights
UI Designer
